About This Site

This has been hand-coded in pure html and css. I took a lot of inspiration from both my own past MySpace and GeoCities site, as well as modern artistic reinterpretations of the aestetic (see Cameron's World).

Many of the GIFs were from there, which in turn came from the old web. I did occasionally lean on copilot for boilerplate and web programming advice, as I am quite rusty in this domain.



About Me

I am me. In the words of Jenny Calendar from BTVS I might consider myself a techno-pagan. In real life I'm an engineer. But really, at the end of the day, I'm just a nostalgic millenial who misses her myspace page, and wants a safe circle for her, well, *circle*, to communicate and gather.



I live in Norway, speak English, Norwegian, and Spanish, and spend my spare time writing, watching 90's re-runs (obviously), walking in the mountains, riding my bike, wearing pink on Wednesdays, and cooking delicious food.



I've done a lot of things in my life. In the time I have left, I want to spend good, quality time with my friends, learn to (better) love myself, and find peace with the sadness around the people that have gone before me.
